Learn how we take care of our plants

At GreenSpace, we meticulously care for our plants by adhering to a structured regimen that ensures their optimal growth and health. Our care routine includes providing the right amount of water, ensuring proper drainage, and offering adequate sunlight based on each plant’s specific needs. We regularly monitor soil conditions and use organic fertilizers to nourish the plants. Additionally, we implement pest control measures and prune the plants as needed to encourage healthy growth and prevent disease. Our commitment to these practices ensures that each plant thrives and contributes to a vibrant and sustainable green space.

Marigolds are commonly referred to as members of the Tagetes genus, and there are several species within this genus, such as Tagetes erecta (African marigold) and Tagetes patula (French marigold).

House plants

Houseplants, also known as indoor plants, are plants that are grown indoors in places such as homes and offices.

They are primarily grown for decorative purposes but also offer several benefits, including improving air quality, boosting mood, and adding life to indoor spaces.

Offer

Indoor plants offer aesthetic appeal, air purification, and stress reduction, while outdoor plants enhance landscaping, provide natural shade, and support local wildlife.
